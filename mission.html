Our concept

Perfair is a webshop where you can buy handmade baby products. All the products are made by struggling mothers from developing countries. By ordering products from Perfair you support these women. Apart from paying these women a fair price for their products, 50% of the companies’ profits go to organisations worldwide that strive to reduce the poverty of young families in areas in Africa and India.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide struggling mothers with a good income. Women will be able to become independent and build a life. We hope this doesn’t only put food on the table for families but also gives children in developing countries a chance of a bright future. In other words: something you buy for your child can mean everything to the children of these young women.

From mother to mother

This is a call to all mothers! Help your fellow mothers and mothers-to-be by purchasing from Perfair. We all know that it takes a village to raise a child and we don’t want to leave mothers behind. You can already help by purchasing beautiful, high quality products that are handmade these powerful mothers.
