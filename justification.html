Justification

Breakdown design elements

Perfair is a brand that sells baby products made by mothers from developing countries for mothers in The Netherlands. We want our visitors to take action after visiting the home page; they have to visit our web shop. Besides visiting the web shop, our visitors can:

• Get informed about our vision
• See the impact of Perfair
• Shop for products
• Get in contact with Perfair
• Visit our social pages
